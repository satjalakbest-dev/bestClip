F5-TTS Voice Reference Files
=============================
Place these files here for F5-TTS voice cloning:

1. reference_voice.wav
   - 5-10 seconds of clear Thai speech
   - Your preferred voice style
   - 24kHz or higher, mono or stereo

2. reference_text.txt  
   - The exact transcription of reference_voice.wav
   - Plain Thai text, no special formatting
